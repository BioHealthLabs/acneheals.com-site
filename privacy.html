Privacy & Cookies

How AcneHeals.com handles data, cookies and affiliate tracking.

1. Who we are

AcneHeals.com is an independent, affiliate-supported comparison website operated by ronarn AS, Norway. We compare acne products using publicly available information and user reviews.

2. What data we collect

We do not collect personal data such as names, addresses or account details. The only data collected is basic, anonymous usage analytics via Google Analytics 4:

  • Pages viewed
  • Clicks on outbound links
  • Device type and country (anonymized)
  • No personally identifiable information (PII)

3. Cookies

AcneHeals.com uses minimal cookies:

  • Google Analytics 4 (anonymous usage statistics)
  • Amazon affiliate cookies (set only when clicking affiliate links)

These cookies do not collect personal data and are used only for: tracking sales, measuring traffic, and improving site performance.

4. Amazon Affiliate Program

AcneHeals.com participates in the Amazon Associates Program. When you click a product link to Amazon, Amazon may place a cookie to register that the visit came from our site. This helps us earn a small commission — at zero cost to you.

5. Third-party links

Our site links to external retailers (such as Amazon). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites. We encourage you to read their policies.
